Reflection Questions



Long-Term Goal/Career Pathway

To be working for (but not limited to) a tech company where I can apply and grow my skills from Dev Academy. I would love to be a part of big projects which will make a big impact to todays society in some way.




Non-Technical Human Skills To Develop

A big thing I would like to learn and take away (non-technical) is how to effectively time manage when coding. I can at times see myself getting lost in the rabbit hole and struggle to know when and where to draw the line.




Strengths and Limitations Description

I feel I have great communication skills, people skills and tend to grasp tech concepts well. However with limitations, I can struggle getting lost in perfecting what I am trying to achieve, at times I can be sensitive to the way things are communicated to me, and I like to make others problems mine too.




Commiting to Manage

  • -Keeping to the time box suggestions

  • -A dairy to plan out my days ahead of time

  • -Communicate my progress regularly to other learner/facilitators and industry representatives especially when I have hit a bump

  • -Not take things personally when it comes to work related aspects

Seeking Assistance

  • -At first instance I will review my work and try and figure out the matter myself and read over instructions and seek online resources.

  • -Failing that I will seek help from fellow students as they may have come across the issue I am facing.

  • -Failing that I will seek help from a facilitator to senior.



Expectations from the Facilitation Team

They explain issues clearly and certain aspects are broken down where needed, to grasp difficult concepts. I would expect that someone would be accessible during normal working hours to ask questions and work around issues.
